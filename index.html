FRZN Fest 2014

Warm up from the winter blues.

FRZN Fest brought a ton of top-notch talent to the High Noon Saloon this year. Besides Superchunk, many of the acts are yet to be on most music fan's radars. Acts such as Lizzo, Caroline Smith, Diarrhea Planet, Nothing, Escondido and more graced the stage over four nights. I was able to cover three quarters of that for The Isthmus.

Read the preview and the review at The Isthmus. See the photos on flickr or facebook.
